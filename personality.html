Discover My Personality

This page delves into the traits and emotions that shape who I am, "Makonnen Gale," my influences, and how they impact my academic life.

My Strongest Trait: Determination and Resilience

Determination and resilience are key traits that define my approach to academic challenges. Whenever I'm faced with a difficult project or a tight deadline, my resilience helps me keep pushing forward, while my determination ensures that I never give up. These traits were evident during a recent group project, where my perseverance was crucial in meeting a tight deadline and producing high-quality work.

My Hobby

My main hobbies are playing basketball and creating music. Playing basketball teaches me discipline, teamwork, and perseverance, while my passion for creating music, especially through drumming, fuels my creativity. These activities help me maintain a balance between the mental challenges of academics and the physical or creative outlets I enjoy, which boosts my overall performance in school.

If you're interested, check out my personality test results to learn more about how these traits shape who I am.

My Hero: Marcus Garvey

"If you have no confidence in self, you are twice defeated in the race of life. With confidence, you have won even before you have started."

Marcus Garvey has always inspired me to embrace confidence and self-belief, qualities that I strive to uphold in my academic life and beyond. His philosophy reminds me that believing in myself is essential to overcoming obstacles and achieving success, both in school and in life.
