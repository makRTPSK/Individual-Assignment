List of Web Pages and Purpose

  • Home Page:The Home page/ Splash page/ Index page; this page of my website gives and introduction to the website.
  • About Me Page: The about me page displays my resume, vision statement, mission statement and career interests.
  • Personality Page: My personality page showcases my strongest traits, my hobby/s my hero and my favourite quote from them and my test results from keirsy Temperament Sorter.
  • Growth Page: The Growth page is a page dedicated to my reflection on the "Twelve Pillars" and my individual personal growth.
  • Storyboard Page: This page showcases the wireframe, layout, features and intent of the website.
  • References Page: Provides references for external resources and works consulted or cited.

Theme of the Website

The theme of the website is professional and minimalist, using a color palette of blues and greens (#17252a, #2b7a78, #3aafa9, #def2f1, #feffff). The site is intended for prospective employers, recruiters, and colleagues interested in my academic and professional achievements.

List of Features

  • Images: Personal profile images and pictures related to my hero (Marcus Garvey) and my personality test results..
  • Navigation Bar: A consistent navigation menu across all pages to enhance user experience and accessibility.
  • Quote Block: Highlighting inspirational quotes, such as your hero quote from Marcus Garvey, adds a personal touch.
  • Text Content: FDetailed sections including descriptions of your personality traits, hobbies, professional aspirations, and other biographical information.
  • Hosting: A link to where the site will be hosted, making it accessible online.
  • Footer: A footer section that includes my name and links to relevant pages.
